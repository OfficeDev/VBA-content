|**Name**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|
| [Application](00245a30-0468-4c6e-aa0a-28c1c6aa2b2b.md)|Returns an  ** [Application](978c2b99-4271-b953-4283-73b5f3d96f41.md)**object that represents the creator of the specified object.|
