
# DataTable Members (PowerPoint)

## Methods



|**Name**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|
| [Delete](9080007e-8851-d056-77ca-288aba14c92d.md)|Deletes the object.|

|**Name**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|
