
# PbMergeType Enumeration (Publisher)

 **Last modified:** July 28, 2015

Constants that specify the type of mail merge, passed to and returned by the  ** [MailMergeDataSource.Type](da6fe525-4a30-5c53-7485-4775d65518a4.md)** property.


|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **pbCatalogMerge**|3|Catalog merge|
| **pbEmailMerge**|4|E-mail merge|
| **pbMailMerge**|2|Mail merge|
| **pbMergeDefault**|0|Default merge|
