# XlCopyPictureFormat Enumeration (Excel)

Specifies the format of the picture being copied.



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **xlBitmap**|2|Bitmap (.bmp, .jpg, .gif).|
| **xlPicture**|-4147|Drawn picture (.png, .wmf, .mix).|
