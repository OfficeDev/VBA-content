# XlCalcFor Enumeration (Excel)

Specifies what should be calculated.



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **xlAllValues**|0|All values.|
| **xlColGroups**|2|Column groups.|
| **xlRowGroups**|1|Row groups.|
