# XlFormatFilterTypes Enumeration (Excel)

Specifies the types of format filters.



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **FilterBottom**|0|Bottom.|
| **FilterBottomPercent**|2|Bottom Percent.|
| **FilterTop**|1|Top.|
| **FilterTopPercent**|3|Top Percent.|
