# PpArrangeStyle Enumeration (PowerPoint)

Specifies whether to cascade or tile the windows.



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
|**ppArrangeCascade**|2|Cascade|
|**ppArrangeTiled**|1|Tiled|
