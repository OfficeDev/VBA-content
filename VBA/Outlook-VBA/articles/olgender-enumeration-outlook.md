# OlGender Enumeration (Outlook)

Constants representing gender.



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **olFemale**|1|Female|
| **olMale**|2|Male|
| **olUnspecified**|0|Unspecified gender|

## Remarks

Used by the [ContactItem.Gender](contactitem-gender-property-outlook.md) property.
