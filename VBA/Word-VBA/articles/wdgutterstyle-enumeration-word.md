# WdGutterStyle Enumeration (Word)

Specifies where the gutter appears in the document.



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **wdGutterPosLeft**|0|On the left side.|
| **wdGutterPosRight**|2|On the right side.|
| **wdGutterPosTop**|1|At the top.|
