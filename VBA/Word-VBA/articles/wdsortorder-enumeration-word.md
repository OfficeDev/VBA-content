# WdSortOrder Enumeration (Word)

Specifies the sort direction.



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **wdSortOrderAscending**|0|Ascending order. Default.|
| **wdSortOrderDescending**|1|Descending order.|
