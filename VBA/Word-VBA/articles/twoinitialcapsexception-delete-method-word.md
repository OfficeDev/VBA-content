# TwoInitialCapsException.Delete Method (Word)

Deletes the specified two initial-capital exception from the collection of AutoCorrect exceptions.


## Syntax

 _expression_ . **Delete**

 _expression_ Required. A variable that represents a **[TwoInitialCapsException](twoinitialcapsexception-object-word.md)** object.
