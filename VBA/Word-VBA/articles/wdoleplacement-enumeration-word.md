# WdOLEPlacement Enumeration (Word)

Specifies the placement for an OLE object.



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **wdFloatOverText**|1|Float over text.|
| **wdInLine**|0|In line with text.|
