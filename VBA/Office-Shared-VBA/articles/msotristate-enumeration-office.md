# MsoTriState Enumeration (Office)

Specifies a tri-state value.



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
|**msoCTrue**|1|Not supported.|
|**msoFalse**|0|False.|
|**msoTriStateMixed**|-2|Not supported.|
|**msoTriStateToggle**|-3|Not supported.|
|**msoTrue**|-1|True.|
