
# Pages Members (Outlook)
Contains pages that represent the pages of an Inspector window.

 **Last modified:** July 28, 2015


## Methods



|**Name**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|
| [Add](4a28aac5-be6f-0892-0fc1-17ded4dff783.md)|Creates a new page in the  ** [Pages](ed4dd77e-b339-7f43-d036-c02daa69d5b8.md)** collection.|
| [Item](f2bdcbfa-33d1-04bd-7ab5-551ea779e648.md)|Returns a page from a collection.|
| [Remove](211b0f26-8193-6c6f-d3c5-4b8495629cf3.md)|Removes an object from the collection.|

## Properties



|**Name**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|
| [Application](16a3c274-be9f-89de-c35f-0099b6169550.md)|Returns an  ** [Application](797003e7-ecd1-eccb-eaaf-32d6ddde8348.md)**object that represents the parent Outlook application for the object. Read-only.|
| [Class](47d8860f-85cd-d650-ca7b-d3fe4e9fd0c0.md)|Returns an  ** [OlObjectClass](33d724b3-df3c-2a7f-a80f-93b66d96f588.md)** constant indicating the object's class. Read-only.|

| [Parent](6840094e-c82a-9323-f40b-a6f5397f27a6.md)|Returns the parent  **Object** of the specified object. Read-only.|
