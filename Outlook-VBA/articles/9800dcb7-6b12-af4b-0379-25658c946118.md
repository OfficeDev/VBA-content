
# TaskItem.StatusOnCompletionRecipients Property (Outlook)

 **Last modified:** July 28, 2015

Returns or sets a semicolon-delimited  **String** of display names for recipients who will receive status upon completion of the task. Read/write.

## Syntax

 _expression_. **StatusOnCompletionRecipients**

 _expression_A variable that represents a  **TaskItem** object.


## Remarks

This property is calculated from the  ** [Recipients](03743284-9753-6cb9-b5cc-20bc5cb3621e.md)**property. Recipients returned by the  **StatusOnCompletionRecipients** property correspond to BCC recipients in the ** [Recipients](774f56b7-4de8-9584-60cd-4fbf361f4c85.md)**collection.
