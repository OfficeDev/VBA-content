
# VisContainerNested Enumeration (Visio)

 **Last modified:** July 28, 2015

 _**Applies to:** Visio 2013 Preview_

Specifies whether to include or exclude shapes from nested containers; constants passed to the  ** [Page.GetContainers](17d9365b-f9ac-85ba-e1cb-cd02ea1a2f22.md)** and ** [Selection.GetContainers](8e04bed5-f9ef-04bf-3013-c6dd623f9f63.md)** methods.



|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **visContainerIncludeNested**|0|Include shapes that are in nested containers.|
| **visContainerExcludeNested**|1|Exclude shapes that are in nested containers.|
