
# Center controls in a form

 **Last modified:** July 28, 2015



1. Select the controls or groups to center.
    
2. From the  **Format** menu, choose **Center in Form**.
    
3. From the cascading menu, choose one of the following:
    
    
    
      -  **Horizontally**
    
  -  **Vertically**
