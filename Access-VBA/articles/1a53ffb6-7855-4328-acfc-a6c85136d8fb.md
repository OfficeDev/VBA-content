|**Name**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|
| [Application](2e4da06d-69d8-7eb0-1eb0-f2fa8a6b20b8.md)|You can use the  **Application** property to access the active Microsoft Access ** [Application](aefb0713-97e6-e2c7-e530-8fd2e1316a55.md)**object and its related properties. Read-only  **Application** object.|
