
# XlStdColorScale Enumeration (Excel)

 **Last modified:** July 28, 2015

Specifies the standard color scale.


|**Name**|**Value**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|
| **ColorScaleBlackWhite**|3|Black over White.|
| **ColorScaleGYR**|2|GYR.|
| **ColorScaleRYG**|1|RYG.|
| **ColorScaleWhiteBlack**|4|White over Black.|
