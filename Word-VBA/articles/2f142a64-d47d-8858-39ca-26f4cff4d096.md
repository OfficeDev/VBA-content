
# StyleSheet.Move Method (Word)

 **Last modified:** July 28, 2015

Moves a style sheet's order of precedence.

## Syntax

 _expression_. **Move**( **_Precedence_**)

 _expression_Required. A variable that represents a  ** [StyleSheet](5e576ff8-c458-f5bd-730d-9db827c4f76e.md)** object.


### Parameters



|**Name**|**Required/Optional**|**Data Type**|**Description**|
|:-----|:-----|:-----|:-----|
|Precedence|Required| **WdStyleSheetPrecedence**|The precedence level.|
